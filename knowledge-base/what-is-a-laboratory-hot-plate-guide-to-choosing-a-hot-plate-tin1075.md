## üõ†Ô∏è Comparison of Hot Plate Surface Materials

Selecting the right top plate material is the most critical decision in your purchase:

| Material Type | Pros | Cons |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Ceramic** | Excellent chemical resistance, high temperature limits, easy to clean. | Susceptible to thermal shock (cracking if cooled too fast). |
| **Aluminum** | Superior thermal conductivity, uniform heat distribution across the surface. | Prone to corrosion from strong acids or alkalis. |
| **Stainless Steel** | High mechanical durability, impact resistant, will not crack. | Slower heat transfer compared to aluminum. |
